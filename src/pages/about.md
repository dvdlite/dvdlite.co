## Mutual Appreciation Society
I appreciate you stopping by! If you have particularly enjoyed my site or if something I posted really helps you out or inspires you, please feel free to [buy me a coffee](https://buymeacoffee.com/dvdlite) or perhaps consider making a contribution to one of my favorite causes, [Ocean Acres Animal Sanctuary in Barbados](https://www.oceanacresanimalsanctuary.org).  Thank you!

## Colophon
This website is artisanally hand-crafted primarily in Massachusetts, USA, using [markdown](https://daringfireball.net/projects/markdown/), [vim](https://www.vim.org), [Visual Studio Code](https://code.visualstudio.com), [11ty](https://www.11ty.dev), and the [eleventy-excellent v2.0](https://github.com/madrilene/eleventy-excellent) starter. The source code is hosted and publicly viewable on [GitHub](https://github.com/dvdlite/dvdlite.co) and deployed using [Netlify](https://netlify.com).

Made with a &#63743; MacBook Pro.
