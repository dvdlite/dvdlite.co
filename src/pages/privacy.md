Privacy -- especially online privacy -- is very important to me.  Therefore, simply put, I completely respect your expectation of privacy. My website does not and will not collect any data or use any third party tracking functionality.  I don't monitor server logs, track page visits, or support webmentions. One tiny exception: A browser cookie remembers whether you prefer light or dark theme.

You will never see an ad on this site, and I don't use affiliate links.  Any products I mention here are products I use or recommend and I receive no financial benefit by discussing or linking to them. I do have a completely voluntary "tip" link to Ko-Fi on my About page. Feel free to ignore it.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact me using one of the methods in the footer below.
